03
Predictive Modelling for Diabetes Using Machine Learning

Classification and clustering applied to CDC BRFSS 2023 survey data (23,000+ records) to predict diabetes risk and segment population health profiles. Tackled severe class imbalance with SMOTE, evaluated KNN, Decision Tree, and Naïve Bayes models, and used K-Means with PCA to reveal distinct high-risk demographic clusters.

04
Heartbeat & Hope — Survival Analysis After Transplant

Logistic regression survival analysis on 23,665 allogeneic stem cell transplant recipients. Achieved 89% accuracy and 0.95 AUC. Boruta feature selection identified renal failure and severe GVHD as the strongest mortality predictors, with a risk stratification tool classifying patients into low, medium, and high-risk cohorts.

05
UK Sponsorship Visa — Data Analysis Dashboard

End-to-end BI project analysing 16 million+ UK study and work sponsorship applications using SQL Server and Power BI. Built a star-schema data model with custom DAX measures for QoQ growth and study-to-work ratios, surfacing nationality concentration, sectoral dependencies, and post-2020 immigration surges.

Power BI · SQL View Code ↗
06
Hessian Fly Resistance — QTL Mapping & ML

ML classification and QTL mapping on 2,649 USDA plant records to predict Hessian fly resistance in durum wheat. An ANN achieved 98% accuracy, with Random Forest pinpointing larval mortality as the top predictor. QTL analysis identified two major loci together explaining over 50% of phenotypic variance.
